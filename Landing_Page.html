About Valorant Agents

Valorant agents are divided into four roles: Duelists, Sentinels, Initiators, and Controllers. Each role provides distinct tactical advantages to the team.

Agent Categories

Duelists

Offensive agents focused on fragging and taking early fights.

Sentinels

Defensive agents who focus on holding the site and protecting teammates.

Initiators

Agents who pave the way for their team, disrupting enemies.

Controllers

Strategists who manipulate the map with smokes and utility.
